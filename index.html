About Our School

D.N.D. Public School, also known as Dr. Narendra Dwivedi Vidya Mandir, is a highly-regarded English-medium school in Khersa, Bidhnu, Kanpur Nagar, Uttar Pradesh, PIN Code 209214. Established in 2000, our institution has been a beacon of quality education in the region.

We are committed to providing a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth. Our school aims to foster an environment where students can become confident, responsible, and successful learners.

With a 4.9-star rating based on 34 Google reviews, we are recognized for our commitment to quality education within a safe and disciplined setting. Our hardworking staff and excellent education system have received praise from parents and students alike.
